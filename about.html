My ideal job

My ideal job would be working for an innovative automotive manufacturer such as Tesla working in research and development. Since I was a teenager and bought my first car, I have been modifying both mechanically and electronically with the best gadgets I can find or to be more precise, what I could afford. Working for a company that develops new technology, gadgets and works to improve the entire field in performance, safety and reliability. I think the need for automation of driving to whatever degree is becoming more noticeable in Australia now with the shear volume of cars on the road and the increasing danger to motorists. The idea that most accidents can be avoided by eliminating human error or human emotion/arrogance is something that will save many lives, reduce driver stress and improve the majorities way of life. With enough experience and contacts in the industry I would like to start my own company in automotive part design and manufacturing and expand from there, helping to fill the void in the Australian automotive manufacturing market.
